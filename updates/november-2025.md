# November 2025

It is time to launch The Advancement. 
After years of effort, an almost two year sprint, and the last six month flurry, the current situation in the US demands a system that is able to give back to the people, and that's what Planet Nine, and thus The Advancement is about.
The plan is to launch an open beta of November 5th, a day of no import for US citizens. 

All the pieces are tested and working, and as I write this on Novemeber 3rd, I'm getting ready to do the full end-to-end test in the stripe sandbox environment. 
Once that works, we'll go "live" for testing tomorrow, and then launch on Wednesday. 

The Advancement is an affiliate marketplace, where everything sold is done so from "bases" and the bases can be used to monetize communities. 
Rather than extract 10-30% of the sales on the platform, it charges a flat 9% rate, which is divided up amongst the participants of the transaction. 
This divvying up happens automagically at the time of purchase, and then left over funds will be disbursed on a tbd timeline.
